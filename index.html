Silvia Orengo

Sound designer and composer with over 10 years of experience in the film and audiovisual industry. Specialized in creating immersive soundscapes and sound design for films, documentaries, and commercial content.

I have collaborated with internationally renowned directors and producers, contributing to projects that have received recognition at film festivals worldwide. My approach combines traditional techniques with innovative sound experimentation, always seeking to elevate storytelling through precise and creative sound design.

Currently working as a freelance sound designer, dividing my time between commissions for film productions, original compositions, and the creation of specialized sound libraries.
